Frequently Asked Questions

How does KaamSetu work?
KaamSetu is a platform connecting skilled laborers directly with clients. Laborers can create profiles, and clients can post jobs. We facilitate secure payments and ensure fair compensation.
Is it free to join?
Basic registration is free for both laborers and clients. We charge a small service fee for completed transactions.
How are payments handled?
Payments are processed through UPI and our secure escrow system. Clients fund the job, and payment is released to the laborer upon successful completion.
